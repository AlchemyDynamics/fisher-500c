Project Summary

The Bernard Edward's Signature Edition Fisher 500-C is a ground-up resto-mod of the iconic 1960s Fisher 500-C stereo receiver. Every unit is rebuilt with modern audiophile-grade components — improving sound quality, correcting known factory defects, and bringing the visual design into the 21st century. This dashboard tracks the production prototype and the resto-modding pipeline for a theoretical limited run of 100 units.
